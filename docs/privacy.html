Privacy Policy

NEXUS New Tab Extension - Your privacy is our priority

Legal • 8 min read • Updated June 28, 2025

Privacy-First Philosophy

NEXUS New Tab Extension is built with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device — we don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Overview

NEXUS New Tab Extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

NEXUS stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API:

  • User Preferences: Theme settings, font choices, time format preferences
  • Todo Tasks: Tasks you add to the todo list feature
  • Focus Timer Settings: Timer duration preferences and session history
  • Smart Date Preferences: Which date insights you've selected to display
  • AI Learning Data: Tab usage patterns for prediction (local processing only)
  • Cache Data: Favicons and fonts cached for performance (stored locally)

Data We DO NOT Collect or Store

  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or personal identifiers
  • Browsing History Storage: We access recent history (7 days only) for shortcuts but never store or transmit it
  • Website Content: We don't read or access content from other websites (except small favicon images)
  • Analytics Data: No usage tracking, telemetry, or external analytics
  • Location Data: No location tracking or geolocation access
  • Search Queries: Search queries are sent directly to search engines, not stored
  • Bookmark Modifications: We read bookmarks for shortcuts but never modify, create, or delete them

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized extension functionality
  • Remember your preferences between browser sessions
  • Enable AI-powered tab prediction (processed locally)
  • Maintain your custom settings and configurations
  • Cache resources for improved performance

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

100% Local Processing: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

  • Encryption: Chrome automatically encrypts stored extension data
  • User Control: You can clear all data by removing the extension
  • No External Servers: We operate no servers and collect no data
  • Secure APIs: Uses browser's built-in security mechanisms
  • IndexedDB Cache: Local caching with automatic cleanup and size limits

AI and Machine Learning

NEXUS includes AI-powered tab prediction that:

  • Processes data locally: All machine learning happens on your device
  • No external AI services: No data sent to OpenAI, Google, or other AI providers
  • Privacy-preserving: Learning patterns never leave your browser
  • User controlled: Can be disabled or reset at any time
  • Transparent algorithms: Open source implementation you can review

Browser Permissions Explained

NEXUS requests the following permissions for specific productivity features. Each permission is used responsibly with minimal scope:

Required Permissions

  • storage - Store your preferences and settings locally in your browser. Essential for remembering your theme, time format, and feature selections across browser sessions.
  • tabs - Create new tabs when you click the extension icon. This is fundamental to the new tab replacement functionality.

Productivity Feature Permissions

  • bookmarks - Read-only access to display your bookmarks in Quick Shortcuts. We never modify, create, or delete bookmarks - only read them to provide convenient access.
  • topSites - Access your most frequently visited sites to populate intelligent shortcuts based on your actual browsing patterns.
  • sessions - Enable "Recently Closed Tabs" feature for quick recovery of accidentally closed tabs. Standard productivity feature for workflow continuity.
  • history - Limited access to recent browsing history (7 days only) to enhance Quick Shortcuts when other sources are insufficient. No history data is stored or transmitted.
  • host_permissions (https://*/*) - Used exclusively for fetching small favicon images to provide visual consistency in shortcuts. No website content or user data is accessed.

Privacy Guarantee: All permissions are used solely for local functionality. No browsing data, bookmarks, or history is ever stored permanently or transmitted to external servers. Everything is processed locally in your browser.

Third-Party Services

Minimal External Connections

NEXUS makes limited external connections only for:

  • Google Fonts: Loading selected typography themes (optional)
  • Favicon Service: Fetching website icons for bookmarks (cached locally)
  • Search Engines: Direct queries to selected search engines (DuckDuckGo, YouTube, etc.)

No analytics, tracking, or data collection services are used.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data in chrome://extensions/
  • Modify: Change or update preferences at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Reset: Clear specific data types through extension settings
  • Disable Features: Turn off AI prediction, caching, or other features

Data Retention

  • Data is retained locally until you uninstall the extension
  • You can manually clear specific data through extension settings
  • Browser data clearing will remove all extension data
  • Cache data automatically expires and is cleaned up
  • No data retention on external servers (because we don't use any)

Children's Privacy

NEXUS does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general productivity use and does not target children specifically.

  • Community Oversight: Open to community review and contributions
