Common Questions

How do I access my bookmarks?

Press K to open the quick panel, which shows your most relevant bookmarks powered by AI prediction.

Can I disable features I don't use?

Yes! Press S to open settings, go to the Features tab, and toggle any feature on or off.

How do I change the time format?

In Settings → General, you can switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats.

Where are my tasks stored?

All data is stored locally on your device for privacy. Tasks persist across browser sessions but stay on your computer.
